# Supermarket-Sales-Analysis-Project
This project provides a comprehensive analysis of sales performance for a supermarket shop, utilizing data across product categories, sales channels, and time periods. The analysis is summarized in a visual Sales Dashboard.

## Executive Summary
  - Total Sales: $401,412 
  - Total Profit: $68,908 
  - Overall Profit Margin: 21% 
  - Total Quantity Sold: 4,280 units

## Key Performance Insights
### Sales by Channel (Sales Type)
Sales are distributed across three primary channels, with Direct Sales being the dominant contributor:
  - Direct Sales: $208,140 (approx. 52%) 
  - Online: $133,924 (approx. 33%) 
  - Wholesaler: $59,348 (approx. 15%)
### Category Performance
  - The inventory is segmented into five distinct categories, with Category 04 achieving the highest total selling value at $95,269.40. This is followed by Category 02 with $92,963.87 and Category 05 with $91,617.19. Rounding out the performance are Category 01, which generated $69,261.95 , and Category 03, which contributed $52,299.51 to the total sales.
### Top Product
The highest-performing product by revenue is Product 41:
  - Total Revenue: $22,952 
  - Quantity Sold: 132 units 
  - Unit of Measure: Ft (Feet)

## Temporal Trends
### Monthly Performance
Sales peaked in January and reached their lowest point in April:
  - Highest Month: January ($41,347) 
  - Lowest Month: April ($26,579) 
  - Highest Profit Margin: April (25%)
### Payment Methods
Customers utilize two primary payment modes, which are split equally:
  - Online: $201,895 (50%) 
  - Cash: $199,517 (50%)
